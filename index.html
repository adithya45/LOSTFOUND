WHAT WE DO

We assist students who have misplaced something valuable on college in an effort to ease some of their stress. There are two sections that you can visit: the section and the other is the FOUND section and the LOST You can ask your fellow students for assistance and let others know what valuable item you lost. If you find something, you can also aid someone else. Each part has instructions specific to it. Additionally, since there are no staff members involved in this process, it contributes to maintaining transparency and strengthening the bonds amongst the children.

Found

Please try to report as soon as possible so that you can help someone in need.

When reporting a found item, provide detailed information about where and when you found it.

Be honest and transparent in your report, and ensure the safekeeping of the found item until it can be returned to its rightful owner.
